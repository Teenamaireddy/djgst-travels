3 Day Vizag Itinerary for Students

Budget Friendly Travel Guide 2026

Visakhapatnam (Vizag) is one of the best budget travel destinations in Andhra Pradesh. If you are a student planning an affordable and fun trip, this 3 day itinerary will help you explore beaches, hills, and scenic spots under ₹3000.

Total Estimated Budget: ₹2500 – ₹3000 per person

Day 1: Beaches & City Exploration

RK Beach

Start your trip with sunrise at RK Beach. Enjoy the sea breeze and click amazing photos.

Kailasagiri Hill Park

Hilltop park with beautiful views of Vizag coastline. Ropeway is optional.

Submarine Museum

A real submarine turned into a museum near RK Beach.

Day 2: Araku Valley Trip

  • Travel by early morning train
  • Visit Coffee Museum
  • Explore Tribal Museum
  • Relax at Chaparai Waterfalls

Return by evening and enjoy local dinner.

Day 3: Hidden Gems

  • Yarada Beach
  • Dolphin’s Nose View Point
  • Local shopping & souvenirs

Total Budget Breakdown

  • Travel: ₹800
  • Food: ₹600–₹800
  • Entry Fees: ₹300
  • Local Transport: ₹500
