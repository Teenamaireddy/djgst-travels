Top 5 Student-Friendly Weekend Trips from Vizag

Budget-Friendly & Fun Travel Guide 2026

Looking for quick weekend escapes from Vizag? Here are 5 student-friendly destinations you can explore without breaking your budget. Plus, learn how an AI travel planner can help you organize your trip smartly.

1️⃣ Araku Valley

Perfect for nature lovers. Take a train or bus, explore coffee plantations, waterfalls, and tribal villages. Budget-friendly travel options are available.

2️⃣ Yarada Beach

A hidden gem with less crowd. Ideal for morning or evening relaxation. Free entry.

3️⃣ Dolphin’s Nose

Scenic viewpoint with breathtaking sea views. Easy trekking and photo spots. Entry: nominal fees.

4️⃣ Borra Caves

Amazing limestone caves near Araku. Entry: ₹50 approx. Perfect for adventure enthusiasts.

5️⃣ Rushikonda Beach

Water sports and peaceful sand. Budget-friendly local food options nearby.
